Rib Rub

Description:

A good rub can elevate any piece of meat.

Ingredients / Tools:

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp Kosher salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp celery salt
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine in large bowl and mix. Store in shaker.
