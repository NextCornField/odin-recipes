Smoked Ribs

Description:

Nothing beats a rack of rib fresh off the smoker. Especially when the meat falls off the bone.

We will be using the 3-2-1 method to ensure delicious ribs. This involves smoking directly for 3 hours, wrapping the ribs for 2 hours, and then sauce for 1 hour.

Ingredients / Tools:

  • 1 Rack of St Louis Ribs
  • Rib Rub
  • Smoker and pellets
  • Apple Cider Vinegar in spray bottle
  • Butcher paper
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat the smoker to 250 F.
  2. Clean up the ribs and dry thorughly.
  3. Apply mustard to ribs.
  4. Apply rib rub to the ribs.
  5. Place ribs on smoker.
  6. After 30 minutes, spray ribs liberally with apple cider vinegar. Repeat 3 times.
  7. Spray apple cinder vinegar in a sheet of butcher paper. Wrap ribs and place back on smoker.
  8. After 2 hours, unwrap ribs.
  9. Brush BBQ sauce on ribs in 15 minute intervals. Pull off anytime between the 2nd and 4th application.
