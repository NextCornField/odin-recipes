Margaritia

Description:

Nothing beats a fresh margaritia insead of that premade stuff. Let's jump into great drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Blanco Tequilia
  • 1 Cup Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1/2 Cup Gran Marnier
  • 2 Squeezes of Agace Nectar
  • Ice
  • Margaritaville Machine

Instructions

  1. Halve limes, then juice.
  2. Measure out 1 cup of lime juice.
  3. Measure out 1 cup of Tequilia, 1/2 cup of Gran Marnier.
  4. Combine all with agave in blender portion of Margaritaville.
  5. Fill up Margaritaville with ice.
  6. Blend and serve.
